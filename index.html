Core features

Everything you need to manage home Bible studies—from onboarding to weekly attendance and leadership reporting.

Church registration & admins

Register your church and create admin users to support user management and oversight.

Group leaders & memberships

Admins create group leaders. Leaders manage membership for their own groups.

Weekly & bulk attendance

Leaders submit weekly attendance (one per week) or bulk upload older data for the year.

Dashboards & reporting

Group leaders see team stats. Pastors/admins see church-wide attendance and trends.

Pastoral visibility

View overall home groups attendance, prayer requests, birthdays, and basic demographics (optional).

Reminders & alerts

Notifications when attendance is missing, including reminders to group leaders.

How it works

A workflow that matches how churches operate—without spreadsheets and manual back-and-forth.

1

2

3
Track attendance weekly (or bulk) Submit weekly attendance or bulk upload older attendance records for the year.
